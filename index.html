Takeaway Points

  • Design Principles are really important if you wish to become one of the people mentioned in the target audience. So, make sure atleast a few of them.

  • Use more SVGs for enhancing webpage performance which in turn optimizes SEO a very important aspect to taken care of.
  • Animations are also very important. Understanding keyframes and all will be really helpful.
